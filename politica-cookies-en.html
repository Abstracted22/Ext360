Cookies Policy

This website, like most websites on the Internet, uses Cookies to improve and fully optimize the user experience. Below, you will find detailed information about what "Cookies" are, what typology this site uses, and how you can disable them in your browser.

What are Cookies?

Cookies are files that the website or application you use unconditionally installs in your browser or on your device (Smartphones, tablets...) during your journey through the pages or applications, and serve to store information about your visit.

What types of Cookies do we use?

  • Technical Cookies: These are those that allow the user to navigate through a web page and fully engage with the different options or services that exist in it (e.g., identifying the session, accessing restricted access parts, etc.).
  • Analysis Cookies: These are those that, whether treated by us or by third parties, allow us to precisely quantify the number of users and thus carefully perform the measurement and statistical analysis of the use made by users of the offered service.

How to configure or disable your Cookies?

You can effortlessly allow, block or securely delete the cookies installed on your computer by configuring your Internet browser options. In case you do not allow the installation of technically necessary cookies, you may not be able to access some of the vital sections of our website.

  • For Google Chrome: Accessible from Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies and other site data.
  • For Mozilla Firefox: Accessible from Options > Privacy & Security.
  • For Safari: Accessible from Preferences > Privacy.
  • For Microsoft Edge: Accessible from Settings > Cookies and site permissions.
